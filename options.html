Tab Settings

Controls where the AI tab opens: next to the current tab, after pinned tabs, or at the end.

New always opens a fresh AI tab. Reuse sends to the last tab you used for that AI service.

Replace overwrites the AI input. Append adds your new text after what is already typed.

Focus switches you to the AI tab. Stay keeps you on the current page while sending in the background.

Plays a short beep on send (useful if Focus Mode is set to Stay).

Hotkeys
Windows: Alt + Q | Mac: Option + Q
Windows: Alt + W | Mac: Option + W
Quick Instructions

1) Select text on any page.

2) Click an AI icon OR press Windows: Alt + Q | Mac: Option + Q.

3) To reuse a template: click a prompt on the right to pin it, then Alt + Q will use it.

4) Press Windows: Alt + W | Mac: Option + W to return to the source tab.

Tip: After changing Tab Settings you don’t need to refresh the page — just reselect text if the popup is hidden.
